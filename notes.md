Phospholipid Biosynthetic Proteins
==================================

Occurs in cytosol adjacent to ER membrane.

- Synthesis
    - GPAT and LPAAT acyl transferases
    - phosphatase and choline phosphotransferase
- Allocation
    - Flippase and floppase
